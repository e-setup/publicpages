Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

Crab Studio ("we") provides this policy to explain our data practices for the game Flipping Chess.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect personal information like names or emails. The only data processed is the Android Advertising ID.

2. Use of Advertising ID

This identifier is used strictly for:

  • Advertising: Serving relevant ads via AdMob.

3. Data Sharing

We may share the Advertising ID with third-party ad networks. These partners are required to use the data only for the purposes stated above.

4. Data Deletion & Rights

Users can reset or delete their Advertising ID at any time via Android Settings.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not collect data from children under 13.
